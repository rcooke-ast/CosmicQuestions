Pub food & British Cuisine

The Cellar Door
On Saddler St., roughly opposite Waterstones (map).
Tel: 0191 3831856, £
As the name suggests, from the outside this just looks like a door between two other buildings.
Rose Tree Inn
A little outside the city past the University Sports Centre (map). Tel: 0191 386 8512
Good food and beer in a countryside setting. Nice combined with a walk by the river banks.
Fallen Angel Hotel
On your left, toward the end of Old Elvet (map).
Tel: 0191 384 1037; £££
Excellent meals in a refined atmosphere. Particularly known for steaks and hamburgers, although with vegetarian options as well.
The Court Inn (Pub)
Behind Barclays opposite the Studentsʼ Union (map).
Tel: 0191 384 7350; £
A wide range of pub food (including some game options). Generous portions.
The Market Tavern (Pub)
On Market Square (map)
Tel: 0191 386 2069; £
Traditional British Pub serving traditional British Food at reasonable prices.

Far-East Asian

Thai River
The Gates Centre, across Framwellgate Bridge (map).
Tel: 0191 597 8774; ££
Very good Thai restaurant with classic Thai dishes and a nice location overlooking the river Wear.
Nadon Thai
Just off North Road up Crossgate, opposite(ish) the church (map).
Tel: 0191 3741157; £££
Funky, modern restaurant serving up Asian Fusion cuisine. Excellent food, good service and large portions.
Zen
Opposite The Court (map).
Tel: 0191 384 9588; ££
A lovely restaurant set in a beautiful Victorian building serving up delicious Thai fusion cuisine. Classy decor and good service make this a popular choice.
In Shanghai
Below The Gates Centre, facing river (map).
Tel: 0191 375 7333; ££
Buffet-style Chinese Restaurant. As well as providing a wide range of choice from the buffet, individually prepared side orders are also included with the meal.
