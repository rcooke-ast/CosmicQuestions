Registration

The workshop will take place on January 6th and 7th 2016 in room OC218 of the Ogden Centre for Fundamental Physics, located on Durham University's science site.

The workshop is planned to start on Wednesday at 11:00am (with coffee from 10:30am) and will end around 16:00 on Thursday.

A buffet lunch will be served in the Bransden Room on both days for all registered participants, thanks to the support of the Centre for Extragalactic Astronomy, the Institute for Computational Cosmology, the European Research Council and the Royal Society.

*Registration will close on Friday 27th November*

Registration

The registration fee of £35 will include the cost of dinner on Wednesday night at Finbarr's Restaurant. Delegates not attending dinner will not have to pay the registration fee. Registration fees will be collected internally by Nathalie Dupin (for Edinburgh delegates) and Lindsay Borrero (for Durham delegates). A £10 deposit is payable by the close of registration, with the balance to be paid no later than Friday 18th December.
